Bank Loan Applications Dashboard
Excel · Pivot Tables · Power Query
14.4K applications $169M funded 84% good loan rate
Problem
Loan operations teams needed a single view of application volume, funding outcomes, and risk exposure to track performance week over week.
Approach
Cleaned and structured 14.4K loan records in Excel, then used Pivot Tables and Power Query to segment applications by grade, purpose, term, employment length, and state.
Result
Published a KPI dashboard showing an 84% good loan rate against 12.30% average interest and 13.35% average debt-to-income, giving stakeholders a clear read on portfolio health.

Blinkit E-Commerce Sales Dashboard
Excel · Power BI · DAX
$1.20M total sales 8,523 items 3.9 avg rating
Problem
Leadership needed visibility into which outlet types, sizes, and product categories were actually driving sales across Blinkit's retail network.
Approach
Built DAX measures in Power BI to break down sales by outlet tier, size, and item type across 8,523 items, with a filter panel for on-demand slicing.
